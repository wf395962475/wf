  • How to research and paper writing, by Prof. Zhihua Zhou.

  • Perspectives on Research Productivity, by Prof. Xinyu Zhang.

  • Some Modest Advice for Graduate Students, by Stephen C. Stearns.

  • How to Write a Good Systems Paper, by Roy Levin and David D. Redell.
